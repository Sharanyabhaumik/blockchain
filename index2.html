About Us

Welcome to Old Is Gold, a place where love and care shape the lives of all elderly people. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where every elder can thrive emotionally, socially, and academically.

Our Story

Old age homes provide many opportunities for socialization, such as group activities, exercise programs, outings, and celebrations. Mental and physical well-being reduce the risk of depression, anxiety, and other health problems.

Why Your Support Matters

n India, around 112 million senior citizens face multiple social, psychological, economic and physical obstacles. It includes both emotional and physical neglect leading to degradation in their health. Many elders, especially in rural areas, are left alone in the house while their children travel the world searching for food, shelter and jobs.
