About Us

Welcome to Orchid, a place where love and care shape the future of every child. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where every child can thrive emotionally, socially, and academically.

Our Story

This is for an orphanage home where thousands of orphans are sheltered, aged from a few months to even in the 30s. After the recent recent flash floods, the shelter has witnessed a major setback in the availability of food items, medicines and other daioly needs and is even running down on the maintainance of the home. They request you all to put a hand forward towards those children who have nobody except this home and you people's love. A humble request to donate as much as you can to help the children.

Why Your Support Matters

The challenges faced by the orphanage, such as insufficient funds for education, healthcare, and daily needs can only be resolved if everyone extends a helping hand towards them.
