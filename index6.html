About Us

Welcome to GoldenLiver, a place where One can easily set up a crowdfunding campaign to raise funds for their loved one's liver transplant surgery.

Our Story

Currently, GoldenLiver, India's leading medical crowdfunding platform has an exclusive donor community of more than 30,00,000 donors who have supported thousands of people for different causes like personal, social, and medical including liver transplants.

Why Your Support Matters

Crowdfunding for liver transplants provides a lifeline for individuals facing the financial challenges associated with this life-saving procedure. It empowers communities to contribute to the well-being of those in need, offering hope, financial relief, and a sense of shared responsibility in the face of a critical medical situation
